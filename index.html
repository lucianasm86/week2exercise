My Favorite Super Heroes

Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman is a fictional superhero in American comic books. The character is a founding member of the Justice League, goddess, and Ambassador-at-Large of the Amazon people. Some of her abilities are: Superhuman strength, speed, durability, reflexes, and longevity.

Chapolin

Chapolin aka El Chapolin Colorado is a latin/mexican superhero. Whenever recognized by the public, Chapolin would boast, stumble and fall right away, proving himself to be puny and timid. One of his fighting skills would be: "La Yegua Voladora". The Chipote Chillon is Chapolin's primary weapon.

Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson is not a former superhero by my personal superhero. Homer's personality lies in his frequent bouts of bumbling stupidity, laziness and his explosive anger. He has a low intelligence level however is described as "creatively brilliant in his stupidity". Homer also shows immense apathy towards work, is overweight, and "is devoted to his stomach".
